About Me

I take ideas that are complex and frustrating, and turn them into simplistic pieces of art. These pieces of art will be seen through mixed media such as logo, signage, business cards, stationery, packaging, media advertising, promotions, and more. Inspired by the famous graphic designer, Paul Rand, I use my art as a bond between me and clients. Its what holds us together. It must not be harsh, confusing, or cluttered. Simplicity is the key to my art style. It’s what creates that bond between those that know and those that do not. Every night I find something new that fuels my interest in my field of study. I look for simplicity, because it is the key to my artwork, using silhouettes , shapes, and colors. With the right imagination, I can create the most powerful image with such simple pieces to the puzzle. This then will deliver the most magnificent product and piece. This is what I enjoy. This is why I do what I do.

Ender's Game DVD

Ender's Game DVD cover

Designing a DVD cover for a new featured film that will be released. The DVD cover design has a very simplistic look, that includes most of the elements of the movie itself. This movie is based off of an old sci-fi novel. With this in mind, I wanted the cover to look uncomplicated yet project a powerful emotional response.

Avvio Beer

Avvio Beer packaging

A package design for a company called Avvio, meaning “Boot”. This Company makes Italian Beer imported from italy. What makes this beer so special is its unique flavors of beer which is projected through the organic asthetic of the package. The case is designed to hold 4 16FL OZ bottles.

Red Drop Soda

Red Drop Soda can

This is a continuous package design for a soda beverage called Red Drop Soda. The product is designed around health and fitness and this idea is translated into the packaging. The varied color pallet and fresh design inspires fitness and fun around the entire can.

Johnny Bruscos Pizza
